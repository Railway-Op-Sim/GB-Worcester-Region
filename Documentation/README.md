# Worcester Region

This map covers the greater Worcester area in the United Kingdom stretching from Kidderminster in the north down to Ledbury via Worcester Shrub Hill, and Worcester Foregate Street.

## Simulation

The included timetable is set in Spring 2025 and includes local services operated by West Midlands Railway, and services towards London by Great Western Railway. All stations and platforms are included.

### Level Crossings

Take note of the three level crossings on the map:

- North of Hartlebury.
- Henwick Road.
- Stocks Lane.

### Reversing Locations

This will very likely trip you up, even the author regularly had to start again due to messing this up. The following table outlines all six of the reverse locations used by services.

|**Location**|**Description**|**Comments**|
|------------|---------------|------------|
|Henwick     |Towards Malvern from Foregate Street.|Use reversing siding.|
|Wylds Lane Junction|Just north of Worcester Parkway (WP) portal.|Use the lower track for reversing.<br>Manually route trains towards WP wrong direction.|
|Signal 54|Just south of Shrub Hill platform 2 towards Worcestershire Parkway.|Manually route trains towards WP.|
|Tunnel Junction|Just north of Worcester towards Droitwich Spa.|Reverse just after the shunt signal.|
|Malvern Wells|Just south of Great Malvern towards Ledbury.|Reverse on lower track (only possible option).|
|Long Sidings|Just south of Shrub Hill platform 1|Reverse into siding from platform 1|

### Additional Tips

- The simulation immediately begins with some activity at Henwick, make sure to route the trains correctly into Foregate Street, and ensure the crossings are closed.
- Be sure to correctly observe the service timetables, many services turn around at Worcester Shrub Hill.
- Worcester Foregate Street has two bi-directional platforms with trains coming from either Droitwich Spa, or Worcester Shrub Hill. Note there is no crossover to the right of Foregate Street so make sure trains are routed correctly entering from the Malvern direction.
- Do not set automatic signals after opening the session, these have already been set and doing so will prevent reversing at Wylds Lane Junction.
- Take care when operating the level crossings around Henwick to minimise delays.
